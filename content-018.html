OK I have seen this exact problem arrive at my shop for repair. The corrosion is inside the optical block with the IR receiver and transmitter in it. This must be removed entirely from the circuit board and the green stuff inside it will be evident. Took me forever to find it and it was one of those eureka moments! Good luck, that module removal is not easy either.
